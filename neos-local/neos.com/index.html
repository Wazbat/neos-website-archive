Neos for creators

Have you ever wanted to create your own virtual world? Neos is built to offer maximum flexibility and creative control. With its powerful engine, Neos provides you with everything you need to create incredible virtual worlds and experiences. Set up your avatar completely in game, collaborate in real-time, import any 3D asset in seconds, and bring your creations to life with advanced visual scripting tools.

Neos Metaverse allows you to:

• Build collaboratively in real time without taking your headset off
• Add advanced interactions with powerful visual scripting language
• Seemlesly toggle between Desktop and VR...

Neos for online communities

Neos Metaverse is the first social VR platform that allows you to import, build and modify your avatar completely in-game. With full body IK and up to 11 tracking points support you can use Neos to dance, party or collaborate with others in a true sense of presence, just like in the real life!

Use Neos Metaverse to:

• Meet with your friends in immersive and fun environment
• Find people with same interests and passions
• Play games and explore vast array of virtual worlds...
