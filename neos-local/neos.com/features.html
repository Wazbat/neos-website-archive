From real-time collaboration to visual scripting

Neos Metaverse is the ultimate solution for real-time virtual reality collaboration, social experiences, and creative development.

Choose from the arsenal of creative tools, brushes, and materials prepared by our community, or build your own. With no-code, node-based LogiX visual scripting system you can make your own tools, simulations, and games right within VR.

Create. Inspire. Amaze.

  • Build everything collaboratively in real time with without taking your headset off
  • Simple creative tools - brushes, material guns, shape tools
  • Advanced building tools - scene & component inspector, editing gizmos
  • Powerful visual scripting language LogiX capable of easily adding custom interactions and building complex systems, games and simulations
  • Import game assets as you work - over 40 3D model formats (including FBX, Blend, OBJ), textures/images and audio
  • Export anything as 3D model and make Neos part of your pipeline
  • Basic in-game texture & mesh processing for common modifications
  • Blendshape support with automatic viseme setup

Play. Share. Have Fun.

Our advanced avatar system allows for importing, building, and customizing avatars completely in-game.
Neos supports enhanced facial and body tracking, haptic feedback, interactive dynamic bones, and a fully configurable, full-body IK system with up to 11 tracking points so everyone can see and react to your facial expressions and body language; bringing you even closer to those you’re collaborating with.

  • Virtual camera - captures photos, stereo and 360 videos with configurable resolution
  • Easy media sharing - instantly import music (including YouTube links), pictures and 3D models
  • Friend notification - get notified when your friends are online and which worlds are in.
  • First person mode gives you the viewpoint of your character, so you can interact with the world with fully functional tools and gadgets.
  • Third person mode lets you see your avatar when socializing or building content.
  • Toggle between Desktop and VR - no restart needed!
  • Advanced locomotion system
  • Walking, sliding, zero-G, spherical gravity and more
  • Teleporters, force fields, springs, portals and custom coordinate spaces
  • Quick in-game scaling - shrink or grow to any scale!
  • Haptics feedback support (bHaptics, OWO Game Suit)

Educate. Simulate. Collaborate.

From visiting planets and galaxies far, far away to virtual CPR training—there's so much that virtual reality lets you experience. Use Neos Metaverse to run field experiments, teach classes, build emotional intelligence and create the perfect learning environment that exceeds any textbook or instructional video.

  • Inventory system - save and spawn anything to any world
  • Have multiple world sessions open at the same time and instantly switch between them just like tabs in a web-browser
  • Fixes and edits can be done within seconds/minutes in-game - no complex and time consuming upload process to go through
  • Private sessions - with P2P hosting and secure authentication
  • Permission system to restrict what others can do in your worlds
  • Effortless self hosted sessions
  • Headless server for setting up custom dedicated servers
  • Volumetric data visualization (e.g. CT or MRI) with slicing and filtering
  • Wide hardware support
  • Full Index Controller support with finger tracking
  • Leap Motion support with full body avatars
  • Eye tracking support (Vive Pro Eye) - look direction, eye closing, pupil dilation
  • Lip tracking support (HTC Vive Facial Tracker)

For a full list of features and supported hardware please visit our Wiki page
